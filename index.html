Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Summary: OMNI is designed with a "Local-First" philosophy. Your voice and text notes are stored on your device by default. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, OMNI may process the following information:

  • User-Generated Content: Text notes and voice recordings you create within the app.
  • Microphone Data: Accessed only when you explicitly trigger the recording function.
  • Device Information: Anonymous diagnostic data (app version, crash logs) to improve stability.

2. How We Use Information

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide core features such as saving and searching notes.
  • To enable AI-powered semantic search and voice processing.
  • To maintain and improve the performance of the application.

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Most data processing occurs directly on your device. We do not have access to your private notes unless you explicitly enable cloud sync features.

Security: We implement industry-standard encryption for any data transmitted for backup or synchronization purposes.

4. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Delete any note or recording at any time within the app.
  • Revoke microphone permissions through your device settings.
  • Opt-out of any optional cloud-based features.
